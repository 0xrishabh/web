#GTC

Here is your Gitcoin token

GTC governs the Gitcoin Network to decentralise Grants, manage disputes and govern the treasury. It will soon prevent sybil attacks by launching a decentralized reputation system. With your help, we are accelerating the flywheel of Gitcoin from web2 to web3. Let’s see what this thing can do.

#Governance

Governance means we all are part of something.

We believe the future of Gitcoin is community governance. GTC is the first step in Gitcoin’s progressive decentralization.

#Quadratic Voting

Nobody can exclude you, you have a voice.

With Quadratic Funding (QF), whales & central power brokers no longer control an ecosystem. Quadratic Funding is a foundational part of the Gitcoin Social contract. We have found QF to be the mathematically optimal way to fund an ecosystem. The Gitcoin team will introduce a proposal to do Quadratic Voting for Gitcoin’s governance as soon as we sufficiently solved for Sybil attacks.

#Information Age

Powering Public Goods in the Information Age

Our deep reason for being is to economically empower builders of the open web .  Open Source Software is a public good. Public Goods Funding is an important unsolved coordination problem in the information age. Gitcoin is solving public goods problems, and we take seriously the precedent we are setting in how to fund Open Source Software development. We aspire to demonstrate a sustainable & decentralized model for building public goods.
