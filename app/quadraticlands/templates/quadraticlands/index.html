With the launch of GTC, Gitcoin begins our second chapter focused on decentralizing from a product into a protocol for Quadratic Funding.

#GTC

Gitcoin’s token is here

To start, GTC governs the Gitcoin Network to decentralize Grants, manage disputes and govern the treasury. It will soon prevent sybil attacks by launching a decentralized sybil prevention & reputation system. With your help, we are accelerating the flywheel of Gitcoin from web2 to web3. Let’s see what this thing can do.

#Governance

Governance means we all are part of something

We believe the future of Gitcoin is a community-centric self-governing protocol for funding public goods. GTC is the first step in our path towards the progressive decentralization of the Gitcoin platform, where each voice of our community can be heard and used to shape our future.

#Quadratic Voting

Nobody can exclude you, you have a voice

With Quadratic Funding (QF), whales & central power brokers no longer control an ecosystem. We have found QF to be the mathematically optimal way to fund public goods. The Gitcoin team will introduce a proposal to do Quadratic Voting for Gitcoin’s governance as soon as we sufficiently solved for the biggest open research problem in Quadratic Voting – Sybil attacks.

#Information Age

Powering Public Goods in the Information Age

Our deep reason for being is our mission – to economically empower builders of the open web. Open Source Software is a public good and its funding is an important unsolved coordination problem in the information age. Gitcoin is solving this problem, and we aspire to demonstrate a sustainable & decentralized model for building public goods.
