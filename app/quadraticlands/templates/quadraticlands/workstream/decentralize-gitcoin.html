Decentralize Gitcoin

Public goods are an important part of building a strong ecosystem, and Gitcoinâ€™s mission is to support digital public goods. But there is a problem! Crypto is a decentralized ecosystem, and the current Gitcoin experiences are crypto-aware but not fully decentralized - We call this Web 2.5.

Gitcoin should not be a centralized pillar of a decentralized ecosystem, and should ultimately be run on credibly neutral technology. From 2021-2025, we will be actively working to decentralize Gitcoin - This means building new web experiences that are fully decentralized, and eventually turning over every property the Gitcoin Company manages to the DAO. Join this working group to help with this effort!

Suggested Quests

  • Build out of L2 interfaces for Gitcoin Grants, and eventually replacing the web2.5 grants experience entirely
  • Create core set of APIs that the community can use to build on top of Gitcoin (e.g. Proof of Personhood Passport)
  • Build auxiliary tools to support our mission (quadraticvote.co, wtfisqf.com)
  • Embrace, partner with & support other QF protocols (CLRFund, Build Guild) and possibly other public goods funding mechanisms (Radicle)
