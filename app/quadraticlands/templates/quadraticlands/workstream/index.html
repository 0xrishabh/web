Workstream

Gitcoin builds Gitcoin

We are working with the community to create groups of stewards who have the mastery, autonomy, and purpose to create GitcoinDAO - the decentralized organization that is building what we call the Quadratic Lands - a better world for public goods and digital democracy .

The time commitment for each workstream is 3-5 hours / quarter to stay in the know (join discord, attend monthly call), and variable based upon what side-quests you go on that are funded by the Treasury.

How to Participate?

1. Choose a workstream

2. Git your bearings - Start showing up to monthly meetings & meeting people.

3. Find someone who has a problem to solve & budget to do it.

4. Do work for the DAO + Git Coins.
