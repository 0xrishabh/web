Own Where You Earn

By Developers, for Developers

Gitcoin is focused on creating impact that lasts, putting the community first, and creating true value with everything we build.

Many of us started our careers as builders, we’ve experienced the horrors of developer recruitment and we’ve been acutely aware that open source software builders were not fairly compensated for the value they create in the world.

We are changing this. Gitcoin is where builders, developers, and protocols come together to build and fund the open web.

Economically Empower Builders of the open web

Our mission is to empower the creators, it’s our reason for being and it underlies everything we do. We believe in an internet where creators own their work and collectively determine their future.

We love builders and digital creators of all shades (designers, illustrators, videographers, writers, artists of all stripes). The diversity and plurality of the community is our greatest strength, and the common thread that binds us all is to give the creators leverage over their financial circumstances.

How We Help

Developers create financial sovereignty with Gitcoin.
  1. Earn – to (git)coins in exchange for their work
  2. Learn – to gain new skills, level up, and create new opportunities
  3. Connect – both to “network,” and to create a genuine, deep, and beautiful community
  4. Own – own a piece of the blockchain based future

Architecture

Gitcoin is a double sided market that connects Developers that want to work in OSS to Funders that are looking for talents to build their ecosystem. This creates a symbiotic relationship that reinforces uniting these two communities.

For ecosystem partners, we deliver a cooperatively built internet through a developer relations funnel.

We then encourage developers to participate in virtual hackathons – a 2 week celebration of technology + entrepreneurship.

Retaining and funding those developers with Gitcoin Grants – the largest crowdfunding platform in web3, and KERNEL – an intimate community seeded by a quarterly 8 week accelerator that mints new web3 projects and technologies, helps developers build companies or protocols that have staying power.

As of 2021, we have built tools that allow developers to earn, learn, fund, and connect with Hackathons, KERNEL, and Grants.

Why are we launching GTC – the governance token of Gitcoin? We are deepening our relationship with the community + solving some governance problems with the network. Our vision is to align the incentives of capital & labour by allowing software developers to own where they earn.

Trusted by Top Web3 Teams

Gitcoin’s community is home to over 400,000 web3 ecosystem builders, a home for events, a source of funding, aligned around one mission. The mission of Gitcoin is to economically empower builders + develop robust creator ecosystems. As of 2021, creators in the Gitcoin network are earning over 5 million dollars each year.
