Possible Statuses:
  1. Open: Bounty funds have been staked onto the smart contract; and are unclaimed by anyone. Bounty is available for a new developer to come tackle.
  2. Claimed Bounty scope have been claimed by at least one developer.
  3. Fulfilled: Bounty scope has been completed, and bounty hunters have *submitted* a claim
  4. Cancelled: The funder has accepted the claim. The staked funds have been paid out.
Cancellation Statuses:
  1. Expired: Bounty was not completed by expiration date and has expired.
  2. Canceled: Bounty was not completed, and was canceled by the bounty submitter.
