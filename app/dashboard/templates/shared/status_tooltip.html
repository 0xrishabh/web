Possible Status:

  1. Open: A funded bounty is available for anyone to work on.
  2. Work Started: Work has been started by at least one person.
  3. Work Submitted: Completed work has been submitted by for review.
  4. Work Done: The submitted project has been accepted and the funds have been paid.

Cancellation Status:

  1. Expired: Work was not completed by the expiration date.
  2. Canceled: A funded bounty was cancelled by the owner of the project.
